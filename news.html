Museum and art gallery exhibitions around the UK in March

The best museum and art gallery exhibitions around the UK in March 2022

This major exhibition brings together William Hogarth’s greatest works with those of his peers across Europe, exploring the parallels and exchanges that crossed borders as they captured a changing society and culture in the mid 18th century. Visitors to the Tate will be able to see over 60 of Hogarth’s best-known paintings and prints, such as Marriage A-la-Mode (1743), The Gate of Calais (1748) and Gin Lane (1751), alongside works by famed European artists including Chardin, Longhi, Crespi and Troost.

New typewriter exhibition at the National Museum of Scotland

The exhibition explores the role of typewriters in society, the arts and popular culture through the personal stories of those who designed, made and used them, Sep 2, 2021

The exhibition explores the role of typewriters in society, the arts and popular culture through the personal stories of those who designed, made and used them

This exhibition currently running at the National Museum of Scotland charts the evolution of these machines, from their inception as curious pieces of technology, to stylish mid-century models that are now highly coveted collectors pieces.

Although computers gradually superseded typewriters, many people still love their unique aesthetic and the special experience of writing on one. The exhibition is will be at the National Museum of Scotland until April 17th.
